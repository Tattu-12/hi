Veg Manchurian Recipe

Ingredients

Procedure

For the Manchurian Balls

2 cups finely chopped cabbage
1/4 cup finely chopped spring onions
whites and chopped spring onion greens
1 cup grated carrot
1/4 cup cornflour
1/4 cup plain flour
2tsp finely chopped green chillies
1tsp finely chopped ginger(adark)
2tsp finely chopped garlic(lehsun)
salt to taste
freshly ground black pepper (kalimirch) to taste
oil for deep frying

For the Dry Sauce

2tsp cornflour
2tbsp oil
2tsps finely chopped green chillies
1tsp finely chopped ginger(adark)
1 1/2tsp finely chopped garlic(lehsun)
1/2 cup finely chopped spring onions
whites and chopped spring onions greens
1 1/2 tsp soy sauce
1tsp vinegar
1 1/2tbsp tomato ketchup
1 tsp red chilli sauce
salt to taste
freshly ground black pepper (kalimirch) to taste
1tbsp finely chopped spring onion greens

For the Manchurian Balls

1. Combine all the ingredients along with 2 tbsps of water in a deep bowl and mix very well.
2.Divide the mixture intp 14 equal portins and shape each portion
into a ball (if you find it difficult to form balls, sprinkle a little water).
3.Heat the oil in a deep no -stick pan and deep-fry the balls, a few at a time, on a medium flame till
they turn golden brown in colour from all the sides.Drain on an absorbent paper and keep aside.

For the Dry Sauce

1. Combine the cornflour and 1/4 cup of water in a bowl, mix well and keep aside.
2. Heat the oil in broad non-stick pan, add the green chillies, ginger,
garlic and spring onion whites and greens and saute on a high flame for a few seconds.
3. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, tomato ketchup, red chilli sauce, cornflour- water mixture,
salt and pepper, mix well and cookon a high flame for 1 minute.

For the Dry Sauce

1. Add the manchurian balls to thr dry sauce, toss gently and cook on a high flame for 1 minute.
2. Switch off the flame, add the spring onion greens.
3. Serve immediately.
